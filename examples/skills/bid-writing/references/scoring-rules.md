# Bid Scoring Rules

Use these rules to check whether a draft response is competitive.

## Compliance

- Every mandatory requirement is answered directly.
- Claims are backed by evidence, examples, metrics, or named deliverables.
- Exceptions and assumptions are clearly marked.

## Buyer Value

- The first page explains business outcomes, not only vendor capability.
- Benefits are specific to the buyer's problem.
- Risks and mitigations are written plainly.

## Structure

- Headings match the buyer's evaluation criteria where possible.
- Long sections start with a short answer, then evidence.
- Tables are used for requirement mapping and responsibility splits.

## Tone

- Use confident, concrete language.
- Avoid unsupported superlatives.
- Keep commitments measurable and auditable.
