# Bid Response Template

## 1. Executive Summary

State the buyer's objective, the proposed outcome, and the strongest differentiator in one short section.

## 2. Understanding Of Requirements

List the explicit requirements and map each one to the proposed response.

| Requirement   | Response          | Evidence                                              |
| ------------- | ----------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Requirement 1 | Proposed approach | Case study, metric, certificate, or delivery artifact |

## 3. Delivery Approach

Describe phases, milestones, responsibilities, acceptance criteria, and risk controls.

## 4. Team And Governance

Name key roles, decision cadence, escalation process, and reporting artifacts.

## 5. Commercial And Compliance Notes

Summarize assumptions, exclusions, dependencies, and compliance statements.
